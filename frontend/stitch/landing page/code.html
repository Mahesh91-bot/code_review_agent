Ditch the generic AI advice. SAGE adapts to your codebase. Whether your team strictly uses const over let, prefers fetch over axios, or has highly specific security guards, SAGE enforces your unique internal rules with zero manual configuration.

1-Click Auto-Remediation

Finding technical debt is only half the battle. SAGE doesn't just leave comments—it generates the exact, corrected code block instantly. Review, approve, and merge fixes with a single click, accelerating your PR velocity.
